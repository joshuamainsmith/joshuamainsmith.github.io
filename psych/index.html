Introduction

Hey, I'm Josh and I'm passionate about learning and creating. I have a B.S. in psychology from Washington State University (WSU) with an emphasis in research. I've worked in a few research labs, including an alcohol and drug abuse lab, a biosciences research facility, and a gerontechnology lab (all on the WSU campus). I'm also a bit of a tech enthusiast (currently pursuing a degree in computer science).
I have training in experimental methodology, statistical analysis (with an emphasis in calculus), statistical programming (R) and software (SPSS), developmental psychology, social psychology, interdisciplinary neuroscience, organic chemistry, and more.
